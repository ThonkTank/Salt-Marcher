# SaltMarcher Roadmap

## Now

- Build encounter planning and management for preparation and table play
  ([#433](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/433)).
- Manage scenes as the current in-game state with relevant NPCs, places, and
  context in one place
  ([#434](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/434)).
- Generate weather for sessions and scenes as part of the calendar and weather
  system
  ([#439](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/439)).

## Next

- Manage campaign flavor for places, factions, NPCs, and events
  ([#435](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/435)).
- Let places, factions, and other context influence encounter pools
  ([#436](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/436)).
- Define loot generation and GM adjustment workflow
  ([#438](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/438)).

## Later

- Manage a music library and scene-based ambience layers
  ([#437](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/437)).
- Support dungeon and hex maps with travel tracking
  ([#432](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/432)).
- Explore a future player app or shared player view
  ([#440](https://github.com/ThonkTank/Salt-Marcher/issues/440)).
