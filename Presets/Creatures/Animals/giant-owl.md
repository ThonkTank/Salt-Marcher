## Traits

**Flyby**
The owl doesn't provoke an Opportunity Attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

## Actions

**Talons**
*Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. 7 (1d10 + 2) Slashing damage.

**Spellcasting**
