## Traits

**Magic Resistance**

## Actions

**Hooves**
*Melee Attack Roll:* +5, reach 5 ft. 5 (1d4 + 3) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, the satyr pushes the target up to 10 feet straight away from itself.
