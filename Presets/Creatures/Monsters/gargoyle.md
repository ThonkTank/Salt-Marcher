*Medium, Elemental, Chaotic Evil*

**AC** 15

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 15 | 11 | 16 | 6 | 11 | 7 |

**Languages** Primordial (Terran)
CR 2, PB +2, XP 450

## Traits

**Flyby**
The gargoyle doesn't provoke an Opportunity Attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach.
