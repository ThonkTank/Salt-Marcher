# Healing Word
Level 1 Abjuration

- Casting Time: Bonus Action
- Range: 60 feet
- Components: V
- Duration: Instantaneous
- Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains Hit Points equal to 2d4 plus your spellcasting ability modifier.
