# Cure Wounds
Level 1 Abjuration

- Casting Time: Action
- Range: Touch
- Components: V, S
- Duration: Instantaneous
- Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger

A creature you touch regains a number of Hit Points equal to 2d8 plus your spellcasting ability modifier.
