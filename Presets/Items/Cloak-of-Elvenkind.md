# Cloak of Elvenkind
*Wondrous (Requires Attunement) Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

While you wear this cloak, Wisdom (Perception) checks made to perceive you have Disadvantage, and you have Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
